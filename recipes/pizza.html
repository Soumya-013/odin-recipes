Margarita Pizza

Description

This classic Margarita Pizza is made with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil, bringing a taste of Italy to your table. It's simple, delicious, and perfect for pizza night!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with sliced mozzarella and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.
